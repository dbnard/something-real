about the album

Manchester five-piece. Debut album recorded in Bergen, Norway with Matias Tellez, produced in part by The Coral's James Skelly. Raw, emotionally charged — taking on mental health, anxiety, male suicide, domestic violence, climate collapse, and society's growing appetite for outrage. The Mancunian lineage, continued.

"The album is basically us saying, 'we're young, we're confused just like you — how did we get here?' It's about trying to find something meaningful in a world that often feels overwhelming." — Cai Alty, frontman
